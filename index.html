Instructions

  • Click the bubble as fast as possible.
  • You can use the FELIX-FELICS to stop the generation of bubbles for 6sec. You can use it twice throughout 1 gameplay.
  • You can also GAUNLET once. It removes half the bubbles present at that time.

  • You can pause/resume game with SPACE
